Research Projects

Sharp Bounding Null Effects in Causal Experiments with Ordinal Outcomes

Proved sharp bounds on the probability of a null effect in causal experiments with ordinal outcomes before and after incorporating prior information on treatment efficacy. Manuscript in preparation.

Bot or Not? Uncovering Social Identity Biases in the Perceived Humanity of Online Profiles

Designed a conjoint experiment to study the impact of social media profile identity expressions (race, gender, partisanship, etc.,) on the likelihood of the profile being flagged as a bot and whether social media users rate users from a different demographic subgroup as bots more often than they do users from their own demographic subgroup. Manuscript in preparation.

Spatial Divide and Conquer for Bayesian High-Dimensional Binary Outcome Models

Scaled up Bayesian hierarchical model-fitting for spatially correlated data with high-dimensional binary outcomes by creating a computationally efficient divide and conquer method. Applied method toanalyzing bird species presence/absence data. Manuscript in preparation.
